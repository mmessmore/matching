Word Definition
Adaptations A community of organisms and their abiotic environment.
Competition The interaction that exists when organisms are needing the same resource.
Producers Organisms that eat organisms that are already dead.
Ecosystem Organisms that break down other organisms and return nutrients to the environment.
Scavengers The variety of life in a particular ecosystem.
Symbiotic A change by which a species becomes better suited to its environment.
Decomposers The relationship between organisms in which one depends on the other. Some examples are mutualism, commensalism, and parasitism.
BiodiversityA group of individuals of the same species that live in the same place at the same time.
Consumers Organisms that eat other organisms and are classified by the organisms they eat for food (herbivore, carnivore, omnivore).
Population Organisms that make their own food by receiving energy directly from the fun (photosynthesis).
